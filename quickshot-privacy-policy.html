Advertising Quickshot Privacy Policy

What information do you collect?

We access information about your Google Ads campaigns and associated performance data via the Google Ads API. We store this data on your private Airtable. We may also retain server logs from our Google Ads API calls which may contain information about your Google Ads account and Google Ads Campaigns. We also store your Client Id, Client Secret, and Refresh Token from Google Ads so that we can automatically download performance data from the Google Ads API.

How do you use the information?

We use your Google Ads data to make insights about your campaigns. These insights result in suggestions such as "Increase spend in Campaign X", "Reduce bid for Campaign Y", etc. We will upload all suggestions to your private Airtable. We will also upload daily performance data from your Google Ads campaigns to your private airtable.

What information do you share?

We upload your performance information and suggestions to Airtable, so Airtable may have access to your data. We use Amazon Web Services to request, process, and upload your data, so Amazon may have access to your data. If we hire third party support or technology contractors, they may be able to access your data as well. We will never sell your data or share it with anyone who is not involved in developing, hosting, running, maintaining, upgrading, or supporting Advertising Quickshot or code and/or data relating to Advertising Quickshot.
